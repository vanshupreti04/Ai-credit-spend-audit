# 📊 Economics Overview

The goal of the audit tool is not just traffic generation.

The real objective is:

```txt
Generate high-quality AI infrastructure leads for Credex.
```

The economics work if:
- users trust the recommendations,
- high-savings companies convert,
- and the audit becomes a scalable lead funnel.

---

# 🧠 Converted Lead Value

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits.

The value of one converted customer depends on how much AI spend can move through Credex.

For the model, I assume:

```txt
Average converted customer value ≈ $4,000/year
```

This balances:
- smaller startup customers,
- larger AI-heavy teams,
- and varying infrastructure margins.

# 🧠 What Must Be True

For the economics to work long-term:

✅ Recommendations must feel credible  
✅ Public reports must be shareable  
✅ Pricing data must stay current  
✅ Lead capture must happen after value delivery  
✅ High-savings users must clearly understand Credex’s value  

---

# ⚡ Biggest Insight

The most important metric is not raw traffic.

The real economic driver is:

```txt
How many completed audits convert into qualified high-savings leads?
```

If the audit consistently produces:
- trusted recommendations,
- actionable savings,
- and qualified infrastructure conversations,

then the acquisition economics become highly attractive.
