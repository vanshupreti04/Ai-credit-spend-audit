# 🎯 Target User

The ideal user is not simply “startups.”

The primary target user is:

- 👨‍💻 Founder
- 🧠 CTO
- ⚙️ Head of Engineering
- 💰 Finance Operator

at a **Seed to Series A startup** with roughly:

```txt
5–50 employees
```

These companies are already paying for:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Cursor
- GitHub Copilot
- OpenAI API
- Anthropic API

but nobody formally owns AI spend management.

---

# 😵 Core Pain Point

These users usually feel:

- 📈 AI spend is growing too fast
- 🔍 Nobody has visibility into usage
- 💸 Teams are buying overlapping tools
- 🪑 Seats remain unused
- 🧾 API bills are becoming unpredictable

The problem is visible enough to feel painful, but not organized enough to justify buying expensive FinOps software.

They want:
- Fast answers
- Actionable recommendations
- No spreadsheet work
- A quick benchmark against similar teams

---

# ⚡ Trigger Moments

The user becomes highly likely to use the product after:

| Trigger | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| 💰 Large OpenAI bill | Immediate cost pain |
| 🔁 Duplicate AI subscriptions | Obvious waste |
| 🪑 Inactive paid seats | Easy optimization opportunity |
| 📉 Runway planning | Pressure to reduce burn |
| 🧠 Founder discussions about AI costs | Social proof & awareness |

# 📈 First 100 Users Strategy

The initial growth strategy is entirely founder-led and content-driven.

---

## 1️⃣ X/Twitter Teardown Posts

Example:

```txt
I audited a fake 20-person AI startup and found $8k/year in wasted AI spend.
```

The goal is to create curiosity and social sharing.

---

## 2️⃣ Share in Founder Communities

Post the tool inside:
- Indie Hackers
- Hacker News
- Founder Slack groups
- AI builder Discords

with:
- screenshots
- public reports
- example audits

---

## 3️⃣ Direct Founder Outreach

DM early-stage founders/operators with a simple message:

```txt
I built a free AI spend audit tool for startups.
Takes 2 minutes and generates a shareable report.
```

The outreach should feel useful, not sales-heavy.

---

## 4️⃣ Show HN Launch

Launch as:

```txt
Show HN: AI spend audit for startups
```

This audience already:
- uses AI heavily
- understands tooling costs
- discusses developer productivity tools

---

## 5️⃣ Public Audit Sharing Loop

Encourage users with:

```txt
>$500/month savings
```

to share their public audit links.

This creates:
- social proof
- organic discovery
- viral distribution

Credex has a structural advantage because the product naturally connects to the business model.

Most SaaS spend tools can only say:

```txt

Credex can additionally say:

```

This makes the audit tool:

The audit becomes the top of the Credex funnel.

# 🧠 Most Important Signal

The most important signal is not raw traffic.

The key validation is:

```txt
Do users trust the recommendations enough to act on them?
```

If users:
- finish the audit,
- agree with the findings,
- and share the report,

then the product likely solves a real pain point.
